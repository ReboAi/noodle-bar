THE NOODLE BAR

The approach to our menu was easy. We had no interest in trying to reinvent food. We went with choices that were popular amongst noodle lovers — basic, down-home style — just from a wide range of regions. We are known for our different types of noodle dishes - Fried noodles, soup bowls, vegetarian, spicy noodles, you name it!
